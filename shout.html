Shout

Social Media Planner

Problem statement

The Fitness industry has suffered a reduction on work due to the global pandemic we are living through. Because of this, trainers need to improve their online marketing skills to keep their business going and growing.
Who is affected: Trainers who have lost the space to do their job and that can’t visit their clients because of restrictions or lack of space.
How might we fix the problem: By giving trainers a platform to create, ideate, organise and schedule content that would motivate their current clients and invite new ones into their business.
Trainers need a service that helps them:
  • Plan what to post
  • Organise their schedule
  • Add their brand and logo
  • Create client-focused content
  • Get content suggestions
  • Increase online exposure
